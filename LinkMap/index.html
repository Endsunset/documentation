LinkMap

Document-based application for homeless feeding activity organization

About LinkMap

LinkMap is a purpose-built iOS app that transforms how volunteers coordinate homeless feeding activities. By combining powerful data management with interactive mapping, it gives organizers complete visibility and control over every aspect of their outreach efforts.

At its core, LinkMap helps you track what matters most: the locations you serve, the items you distribute, and the people who make it happen. Every deployment, location, and volunteer is connected through an intuitive map-based interface, making complex coordination feel simple.

Teams stay connected with built-in communication tools, and event sign-ups keep everyone aligned. Whether you're managing a single route or coordinating multiple teams across the city, LinkMap provides the structure you need to focus on what really matters—helping those in need.

Integrated Map Visualization

Map integration with advanced interaction for location tracking and route planning.

All data stored in structured .linkmap files for consistency and reliability.
